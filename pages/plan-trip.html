Best Time to Visit

Uttarakhand offers different experiences throughout the year. Choose the perfect time based on your interests.

Seasons:

  • Summer (March-June) - Ideal for hill stations
  • Monsoon (July-September) - Lush green landscapes
  • Autumn (October-November) - Pleasant weather
  • Winter (December-February) - Snow and festivals

Travel Tips

Make the most of your trip with these essential travel tips for Uttarakhand.

Important Tips:

  • Carry warm clothing even in summer
  • Stay hydrated at high altitudes
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Book accommodations in advance
  • Carry necessary permits for restricted areas

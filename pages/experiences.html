Adventure Experiences

Embark on thrilling adventures in the majestic mountains of Uttarakhand

River Rafting

Experience the thrill of white water rafting in the Ganges River. Rishikesh offers some of the best rafting experiences in India with rapids ranging from Grade I to Grade V.

Best Locations:

  • Rishikesh - Most popular rafting destination
  • Alaknanda River - Challenging rapids
  • Bhagirathi River - Scenic routes

Trekking & Hiking

Explore the pristine Himalayan trails with trekking adventures suitable for all levels. From easy day hikes to challenging multi-day treks.

Popular Treks:

  • Valley of Flowers Trek - UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Kedarnath Trek - Spiritual journey
  • Har Ki Dun - Valley of Gods
  • Roopkund - Mystery Lake Trek

Paragliding

Soar through the skies and witness breathtaking aerial views of the Himalayas. Uttarakhand offers some of the best paragliding spots in India.

Best Spots:

  • Nainital - Scenic lake views
  • Mussoorie - Mountain landscapes
  • Pithoragarh - High altitude flying
